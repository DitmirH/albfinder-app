LOCAL OLLAMA MODELS TO TEST
============================

1. qwen3-coder-next:latest (51 GB)
   - Newest Qwen model, likely best quality
   - Expected: Highest accuracy, slower (10-20s)

2. qwen2.5-coder:14b (9 GB)
   - Good balance of speed and quality
   - Expected: Good accuracy, faster (3-8s)

3. gpt-oss:120b (65 GB)
   - Very large model, may be overkill
   - Expected: Very high quality, slowest (20-40s)

4. mistral:latest (4.4 GB)
   - Fast baseline model
   - Expected: Decent quality, fastest (2-5s)

RUN COMPARISON:
===============
python compare_local_models.py

This will test all 4 models on the same REIBANQ LTD record and compare:
- Data points extracted
- Accuracy vs known correct data
- Speed
- Which model finds the most contact details
